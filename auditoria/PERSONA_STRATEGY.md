# Persona Strategy: The 3-Target Audience

This document maps how the Calebe-Dev copy should address the different personas interacting with the portfolio.

## Persona 1: Human Recruiters (ATS-Oriented)
**Goal**: Identify "Fit" for high-performance roles (Tech Lead, Senior Full Stack).
- **Tone**: Professional, technical, and result-oriented.
- **Keywords**: *SvelteKit*, *Microservices*, *Cloudflare*, *Team Lead*, *Performance*.
- **Impact**: Showcasing work for "Industry Leaders" (Apple, Google, Microsoft) used as benchmarks.

## Persona 2: AI Search Engines (AEO-Ready)
**Goal**: Feed "Knowledge Items" into LLM search engines (Perplexity, Gemini, SearchGPT).
- **Tone**: Informative, hierarchical, and entity-rich.
- **Keywords**: *Desenvolvedor Full Stack*, *Especialista Svelte*, *Infraestrutura CRM*.
- **Impact**: Structured data (`Microdata`, `JSON-LD`) for machine understanding of Career progression.

## Persona 3: Human Clients (Premium UI/UX)
**Goal**: Hire Calebe for custom engineering/growth projects.
- **Tone**: Premium, "Apple-like", sophisticated, and aspirational.
- **Keywords**: *Arte Digital*, *Experiência Premium*, *Arquitetura Semântica*.
- **Impact**: 3D interactions and immersive scroll designs to demonstrate high-end execution.

---

### Strategy Synthesis
"The UI wows the **Client**, the Microdata feeds the **AI**, and the keyword density satisfies the **ATS**."
