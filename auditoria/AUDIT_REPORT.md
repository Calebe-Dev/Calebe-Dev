# Detailed Content Audit: SEO, ATS & Copy Analysis

This report analyzes every professional text in the project, identifying gaps and proposing high-impact refinements for Recruiting (ATS), AI Engines (AEO), and Human conversion.

## 1. [AboutSection](file:///Users/calebearaujo/GIT/Pessoal/Calebe-Dev/src/lib/components/sections/AboutSection.svelte)
- **Current Status**: Premium and abstract ("Engenharia de Software & Arte Digital").
- **ATS Analysis**: Low keyword density for "Full Stack" and "Tech Lead" in the primary heading.
- **AEO Analysis**: The text is poetic but needs a more direct "Entity" definition for AI understanding.
- **Proposed Change**: Refine the summary to explicitly mention the core stack (SvelteKit, TS, Node) and the "Tech Lead" authority.

## 2. [ExperienceSection](file:///Users/calebearaujo/GIT/Pessoal/Calebe-Dev/src/lib/components/sections/ExperienceSection.svelte)
